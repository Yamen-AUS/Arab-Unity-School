Official Inspection

DSIB Inspection Report 2023–2024

Inspected 27 November – 01 December 2023 by the Dubai Schools Inspection Bureau (DSIB). Key findings from the official report:

Teaching & Learning
Good

Teachers demonstrate strong subject knowledge and use a range of engaging strategies to develop students' skills and understanding across all phases.

Students' Achievement
Good

Students make good progress across subjects and phases. Performance in Cambridge IGCSE and A-Level examinations reflects strong academic preparation and consistent teaching quality.

Students' Personal Development
Very Good

Students demonstrate excellent attitudes to learning, strong moral values, and a clear sense of cultural identity. Behaviour throughout the school is very good.

Leadership & Management
Good

Leaders have a clear and ambitious vision for the school. Self-evaluation is accurate and improvement planning leads to positive outcomes for students.

Post-16 Provision
Very Good

Sixth Form students benefit from expert teaching and broad subject choice. A-Level outcomes are strong, with graduates securing places at leading universities worldwide.

Inclusion & Wellbeing
Good

AUS maintains a caring, inclusive environment for all learners. Student wellbeing is a clear priority with dedicated pastoral care and specialist support provision.

Source: DSIB Inspection Report AY 2023–2024 · Arab Unity School L.L.C · UK Curriculum
