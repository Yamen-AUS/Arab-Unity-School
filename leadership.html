CEO / Principal

Kelvin Hornsby

For over 20 years, Kelvin has served as Principal/CEO, Education Director, and Senior Vice President across state and international private education, leading single schools and portfolios of up to 18,000 students. Since 2016, he has led significant improvement, including outstanding inspection outcomes, world-leading examination results, and sustained growth across multiple schools and curricula. His responsibilities have spanned strategic leadership, people, operations, customer experience, and finance, with a strong focus on significant value add and world-class provision. He has led High Performance Learning, supporting 4,500 educators and over 55,000 learners, always with a people-centred belief in every learner's potential.
