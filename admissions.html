Step by Step

Admissions Timeline

Our friendly team guides every family through a straightforward, welcoming enrolment process.

1

Enquire Online or In Person

Complete the form below or call +971 (0)4 288 6226 to speak with our admissions team.

2

School Tour & Assessment

Visit the school and your child will complete a short, friendly age-appropriate assessment.

3

Offer of Place

Subject to a suitable place being available, you will receive a formal offer within 2–3 working days.

4

Enrolment & Fee Payment

Accept the offer, complete enrolment forms and pay registration/first term fees to secure the place.

5

Welcome Day

Your child is welcomed by their class teacher, form tutor and the wider AUS community.
